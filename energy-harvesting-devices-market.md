<p><p>Energy harvesting devices are crucial in converting ambient energy into usable electrical power. The light energy harvesting market focuses on solar cells that capture sunlight. Vibration harvesting devices convert mechanical energy from vibrations into electricity. Thermal energy harvesting utilizes temperature differentials to generate power, often through thermoelectric generators. Finally, electromagnetic or radio frequency (RF) energy harvesting captures energy from radio waves and electromagnetic fields, making it vital for powering low-energy devices in wireless applications and IoT networks.</p></p>

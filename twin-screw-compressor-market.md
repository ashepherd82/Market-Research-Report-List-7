<p><p>The Twin Screw Compressor market is primarily divided into two types: Oil-Free Twin Screw Compressors and Oil-Injected Twin Screw Compressors. Oil-Free Twin Screw Compressors are designed for applications requiring high purity air, as they eliminate the risk of oil contamination. They are commonly used in pharmaceutical and food industries. In contrast, Oil-Injected Twin Screw Compressors utilize oil for lubrication and cooling, which enhances efficiency and durability, making them suitable for industrial applications where oil contamination is not a concern.</p></p>

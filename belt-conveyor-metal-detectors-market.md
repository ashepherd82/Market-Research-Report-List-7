<p><p>The Belt Conveyor Metal Detectors market comprises two primary types: Flat Metal Detectors and Pipeline Metal Detectors. Flat Metal Detectors are designed for use in conveyor systems, effectively identifying metallic contaminants in bulk materials during processing or transportation. They are commonly used in various industries, including food and pharmaceuticals. Pipeline Metal Detectors, on the other hand, are integrated into pipelines and are essential for detecting metal during the transfer of liquids or powders, ensuring product integrity and safety in production lines.</p></p>

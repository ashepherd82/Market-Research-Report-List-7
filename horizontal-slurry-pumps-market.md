<p><p>The horizontal slurry pumps market is segmented into three main types based on duty classification: light duty, medium duty, and heavy duty. Light duty horizontal slurry pumps are designed for applications with lower solids concentrations and less abrasive materials, suitable for industries like chemical processing. Medium duty pumps handle more abrasive slurries, often found in mining and mineral processing. Heavy duty horizontal slurry pumps are built for the most challenging environments, managing high solids concentrations and abrasive content, typically in heavy mining and dredging applications.</p></p>

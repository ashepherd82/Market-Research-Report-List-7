<p><p>The thyroid function tests market is segmented into three main types: TSH tests, FT4 tests, and FT3 tests. TSH (Thyroid-Stimulating Hormone) tests measure the hormone that regulates thyroid activity, indicating potential hypothyroidism or hyperthyroidism. FT4 (Free Thyroxine) tests assess the level of free thyroxine in the blood, reflecting thyroid hormone production. FT3 (Free Triiodothyronine) tests evaluate the free triiodothyronine levels, providing insight into thyroid function and metabolism. Each test plays a crucial role in diagnosing and managing thyroid disorders.</p></p>

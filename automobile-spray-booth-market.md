<p><p>The automobile spray booth market features several types, each designed to enhance painting efficiency and quality. Cross flow paint booths allow air to move horizontally, providing even coating but limited filtration. Downdraft paint booths have air supplied from the top, enhancing airflow control and minimizing overspray. Side downdraft booths combine side airflow with downward exhaust, improving paint application and reducing contaminants. Other types include semi-downdraft and push-pull booths, each tailored for specific painting needs and environments in automotive refinishing.</p></p>

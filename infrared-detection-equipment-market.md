<p><p>The infrared detection equipment market comprises several key types, each serving distinct applications. Mercury cadmium telluride (MCT) detectors offer high sensitivity at various wavelengths, ideal for military and space applications. Indium gallium arsenide (InGaAs) detectors excel in short-wave infrared detection, commonly used in telecommunications. Pyroelectric detectors are effective for dynamic thermal changes, suitable for motion detection. Thermopiles convert thermal energy into electrical signals, often used in temperature sensors. Microbolometers, with their uncooled operation, are popular in thermal imaging devices, providing real-time heat detection.</p></p>

<p><p>The fuel dual injector market encompasses various types designed to optimize engine performance and efficiency. Direct injection type injects fuel directly into the combustion chamber, enhancing power and fuel efficiency. Common rail type utilizes a shared fuel rail that allows multiple injectors to operate with high precision, improving combustion efficiency across different engine speeds. Sequential type injectors operate individually, timed for optimal fuel delivery, ensuring better control over the combustion process and further enhancing engine responsiveness and efficiency.</p></p>

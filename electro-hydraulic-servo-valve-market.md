<p><strong>The Electro Hydraulic Servo Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Nozzle Flapper Valve</li><li>Jet Action Valve</li><li>Direct Drive Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electro-hydraulic servo valve market includes three main types: nozzle flapper valves, jet action valves, and direct drive valves. Nozzle flapper valves utilize a flapper mechanism to control fluid flow via nozzle pressure variations, providing precise control. Jet action valves operate by directing high-velocity jets of fluid to achieve rapid response and high accuracy. Direct drive valves employ an electrical actuator connected directly to the valve, offering superior performance and precise control with minimal lag, ideal for advanced automation systems.</p></p>

<p><p>Thermopile array modules are devices that detect infrared radiation and convert it into temperature readings. The market is segmented into two primary types: digital array modules and analog array modules. Digital array modules provide processed and calibrated output signals, making them suitable for integration into advanced systems for precise measurements. In contrast, analog array modules offer continuous voltage outputs in response to infrared signals, catering to applications requiring simpler, real-time data without extensive signal processing. Both serve diverse applications in temperature sensing and thermal imaging.</p></p>

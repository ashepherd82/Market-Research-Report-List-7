<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Urea-Formaldehyde (UF) resins are categorized into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I UF resins are primarily used in interior applications, such as cabinetry and furniture, due to their lower levels of formaldehyde emissions. Type II UF resins, on the other hand, are designed for exterior applications and have enhanced moisture resistance, making them suitable for particleboards and plywood exposed to varying environmental conditions. Both types cater to different market needs, focusing on performance and safety standards.</p></p>

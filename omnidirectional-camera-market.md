<p><strong>The Omnidirectional Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Catadioptric Omnidirectional Camera</li><li>Dioptric Omnidirectional Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The omnidirectional camera market is primarily divided into two types: catadioptric and dioptric cameras. Catadioptric omnidirectional cameras utilize a combination of lenses and mirrors to capture a 360-degree field of view, offering high image quality and improved low-light performance. In contrast, dioptric omnidirectional cameras rely solely on lenses to achieve wide-angle coverage, typically presenting a more compact and cost-effective solution. Both types are widely used in applications such as surveillance, virtual reality, and autonomous vehicles.</p></p>

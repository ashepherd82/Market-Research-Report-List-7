<p><p>The pneumatic riveter market is categorized into two main types: stationary and portable pneumatic riveters. Stationary pneumatic riveters are designed for fixed installations in manufacturing settings, offering high efficiency for mass production of riveted joints. They typically handle larger components and higher volumes. In contrast, portable pneumatic riveters are versatile tools that can be easily maneuvered for various applications, making them ideal for maintenance work, field repairs, and low-volume production where mobility is essential. Both types enhance productivity and precision in rivet assembly.</p></p>

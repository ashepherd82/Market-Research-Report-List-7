<p><p>The solids separator market comprises various technologies used to separate solids from liquids in industrial processes. Centrifugal separators utilize high-speed rotation to exert gravitational force, effectively separating solids based on density differences. Magnetic separators employ magnetic fields to extract ferromagnetic materials from mixtures. Gravity separators rely on the natural settling of solids in liquid due to gravity, while other methods may include filtration or screening techniques. Each type serves distinct applications in sectors like waste management, mining, and food processing.</p></p>

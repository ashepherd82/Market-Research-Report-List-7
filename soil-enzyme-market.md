<p><p>The soil enzyme market comprises various types categorized based on their functions. Hydrolytic enzymes break down complex organic materials into simpler compounds, facilitating nutrient availability. Oxidoreductases mediate redox reactions, playing a crucial role in carbon and nitrogen cycles. Transferases are involved in transferring functional groups between molecules, influencing metabolic processes. Lastly, lyases catalyze the addition or removal of groups to create double bonds, impacting soil structure and health. Each type contributes significantly to soil fertility and ecosystem sustainability.</p></p>

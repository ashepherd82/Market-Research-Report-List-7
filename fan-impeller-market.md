<p><p>The fan impeller market consists of three main types: closed, semi-open, and open impellers. Closed impellers have enclosed blades, providing high efficiency and excellent pressure. Semi-open impellers feature blades exposed partially, allowing for better handling of solids yet sacrificing some efficiency. Open impellers consist of blades without a shroud, suited for applications with larger particles but offering lower efficiency and pressure capabilities. Each type is designed to meet specific operational demands in various industrial applications.</p></p>

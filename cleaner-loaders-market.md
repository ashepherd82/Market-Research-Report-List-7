<p><p>The cleaner-loaders market encompasses three primary types: self-propelled, trailed, and mounted. Self-propelled cleaner-loaders feature their own mobility, allowing them to operate independently in various agricultural settings. Trailed models are designed to be towed behind a tractor, offering flexibility and ease of use for larger operations. Mounted cleaner-loaders are attached to the tractor, providing compactness and maneuverability in tighter spaces. Each type caters to different operational needs, enhancing efficiency in grain handling and crop processing.</p></p>

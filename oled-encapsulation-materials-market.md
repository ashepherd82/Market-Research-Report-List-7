<p><p>The OLED encapsulation materials market comprises several types, each catering to diverse application needs. Rigid glass offers strong protection and high transparency but lacks flexibility. Flexible glass provides a lightweight and durable option suitable for bendable devices. Conformal coatings are thin, protective layers that conform to surfaces, enhancing flexibility. Metal foils deliver robust protection against moisture and gases, while laminates combine various materials to optimize performance and barrier properties, making them ideal for various OLED applications.</p></p>

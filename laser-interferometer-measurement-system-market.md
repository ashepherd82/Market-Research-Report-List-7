<p><ul><li>Homodyne Type</li><li>Heterodyne Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Laser Interferometer Measurement System market can be categorized into two main types: homodyne and heterodyne systems. Homodyne interferometers utilize a single laser frequency, measuring phase changes directly, making them ideal for high-precision applications. In contrast, heterodyne systems employ two different frequencies, allowing for enhanced measurement range and resolution through beat frequencies. Both types serve various industries, including aerospace, automotive, and precision engineering, meeting diverse demands for accuracy and performance in measurement applications.</p></p>

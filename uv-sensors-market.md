<p><p>The UV sensors market is categorized into three main types based on the wavelength of ultraviolet radiation: UVA, UVB, and UVC. UVA sensors detect long-wave UV radiation, primarily responsible for skin aging and damage. UVB sensors monitor medium-wave UV radiation, which can cause sunburn and is linked to skin cancer. UVC sensors focus on short-wave radiation, often used in germicidal applications. Each type serves distinct purposes across industries such as healthcare, environmental monitoring, and consumer products, catering to specific safety and health needs.</p></p>

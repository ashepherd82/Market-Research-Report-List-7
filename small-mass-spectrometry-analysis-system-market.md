<p><ul><li> Static Mass Spectrometer</li><li>Dynamic Mass Spectrometer </li></ul></p>
<p><p>The small mass spectrometry analysis system market includes two primary types: static and dynamic mass spectrometers. Static mass spectrometers measure the mass-to-charge ratio of ions in a stable environment, providing high-resolution results for various applications such as environmental testing and pharmaceutical analysis. In contrast, dynamic mass spectrometers allow real-time analysis, offering rapid measurements through continuous ionization and detection. This capability benefits industries requiring timely data, such as clinical diagnostics and food safety, enhancing overall analytical efficiency.</p></p>

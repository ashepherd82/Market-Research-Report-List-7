<p><strong>The Dual Band Wireless Router Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>802.11a</li><li>802.11b</li><li>802.11g</li><li>802.11n</li><li>802.11ac</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Dual Band Wireless Router market consists of various Wi-Fi standards categorized by their specifications. 802.11a operates in the 5 GHz band, offering faster speeds but limited range. 802.11b uses 2.4 GHz, providing broader coverage but lower speeds. 802.11g combines the two, enhancing speed and range. 802.11n further improves performance with MIMO technology, allowing multiple simultaneous data streams. 802.11ac, primarily in the 5 GHz band, delivers higher speeds and capacity, making it ideal for high-bandwidth applications.</p></p>

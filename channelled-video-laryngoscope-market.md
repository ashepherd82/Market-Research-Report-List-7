<p><p>The channelled video laryngoscope market consists of two primary types: rigid fiberoptic laryngoscopes and flexible fiberoptic laryngoscopes. Rigid fiberoptic laryngoscopes feature a rigid design with a built-in camera, providing clear visualization for intubation in difficult airway situations. In contrast, flexible fiberoptic laryngoscopes utilize a flexible shaft, allowing easier navigation around anatomical obstructions, making them suitable for both adult and pediatric patients. Both types enhance airway management by offering improved visualization and maneuverability during procedures.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Welding Helmet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Welding Helmet</li><li>Auto Darkening Welding Helmets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The welding helmet market is primarily divided into two types: passive welding helmets and auto darkening welding helmets. Passive welding helmets have a fixed dark lens that provides consistent protection against harmful light and UV rays, requiring the user to lift the helmet when not welding. In contrast, auto darkening welding helmets feature a filter that automatically adjusts its tint based on the intensity of the welding arc, offering greater convenience and visibility, enhancing comfort and safety during welding tasks.</p></p>

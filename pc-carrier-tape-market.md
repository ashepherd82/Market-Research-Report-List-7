<p><ul><li>Black Conductive</li><li>Transparent Antistatic</li><li>Transparent Non-antistatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PC carrier tape market is segmented into three main types: Black Conductive, Transparent Antistatic, and Transparent Non-antistatic. Black Conductive tapes are designed to dissipate static electricity, making them ideal for sensitive electronic components. Transparent Antistatic tapes provide visibility while preventing static buildup, suitable for applications requiring both protection and inspection. Transparent Non-antistatic tapes offer visual clarity without static protection, often used for non-sensitive components. Each type serves unique needs in electronics manufacturing and packaging, contributing to efficient production processes.</p></p>

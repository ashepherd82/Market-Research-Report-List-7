Acrylic tapes offer strong adhesion and resistance to UV light and moisture, making them suitable for outdoor applications. Rubber tapes provide excellent initial tack and flexibility, ideal for general-purpose use and temporary applications. Silicone tapes excel in high-temperature resistance and are often used in specialized industries like electronics and automotive. Each type serves unique applications based on adhesion strength, environmental resistance, and durability needs.</p></p>

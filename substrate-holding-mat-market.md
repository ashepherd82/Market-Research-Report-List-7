Intumescent mats expand when exposed to heat, providing enhanced fire protection by forming a barrier against flames. Non-Intumescent mats, on the other hand, do not expand but offer stable support and insulation under typical conditions. Both types are crucial in various applications, including construction and insulation, catering to specific needs for thermal management and fire safety in diverse environments.</p></p>

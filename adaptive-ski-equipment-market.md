<p><strong>The Adaptive Ski Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monoski</li><li>Dualski</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The adaptive ski equipment market primarily comprises two types: monoski and dualski. Monoski is designed for individuals with mobility challenges, featuring a single ski that allows for easier control and balance, often used with a sit-down harness. In contrast, dualski consists of two skis joined together, providing greater stability and support for skiers who can control their upper body but may have difficulty with traditional skis. Both options enhance accessibility and enjoyment in skiing for diverse abilities.</p></p>

<p><p>The pneumatic tool oil market is categorized into two main types based on viscosity: low viscosity and high viscosity. Low viscosity pneumatic tool oil is designed for rapid flow and easy dispersion, making it suitable for high-speed, intricate tools that require quick lubrication and minimal resistance. In contrast, high viscosity pneumatic tool oil provides thicker lubrication, offering enhanced protection and performance for heavy-duty tools operating under high stress and load conditions. Both types are essential for optimizing tool lifespan and efficiency.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Synthetic Rubber Based Adhesive (SBR) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-Based Adhesive</li><li>Waterborne Adhesive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The synthetic rubber-based adhesive (SBR) market comprises various types, including solvent-based adhesives and waterborne adhesives. Solvent-based adhesives contain organic solvents and offer strong bonding properties, making them ideal for applications in automotive and construction industries. They provide quick drying times but can emit volatile organic compounds. In contrast, waterborne adhesives use water as a solvent, making them more environmentally friendly and safer to use. They are increasingly popular in packaging, woodworking, and textiles due to their ease of application and lower harmful emissions.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Single Mode Optical Modules</li><li>Multi Mode Optical Modules</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optics module market is divided into single mode and multi-mode optical modules. Single mode optical modules utilize a narrow beam of light, allowing for long-distance data transmission with minimal signal loss, making them ideal for telecommunication and high-speed networks. In contrast, multi-mode optical modules use a broader beam and are suitable for shorter distances, typically within buildings or campuses, offering higher bandwidth at lower costs. Both types cater to distinct networking needs, enhancing data communication efficiency.</p></p>

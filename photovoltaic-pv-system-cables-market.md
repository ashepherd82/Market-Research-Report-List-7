<p><p>The photovoltaic (PV) system cables market comprises DC and AC cables, each serving distinct roles in solar energy systems. DC cables are essential for transmitting direct current from solar panels to inverters, ensuring efficient energy transfer with minimal losses. In contrast, AC cables carry alternating current from inverters to the electrical grid or end-users, facilitating the distribution of solar-generated electricity. Both types are crucial for optimizing system performance, enhancing safety, and ensuring compliance with regulatory standards in solar installations.</p></p>

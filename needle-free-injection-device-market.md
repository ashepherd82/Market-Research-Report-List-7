<p><ul><li>Powder Injections</li><li>Liquid Injections</li><li>Depot or Projectile Injection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The needle-free injection device market comprises three main types: powder injections, liquid injections, and depot or projectile injections. Powder injections utilize compressed air to deliver medication in powder form, allowing for rapid absorption. Liquid injections use high-pressure mechanisms to deliver liquids without needles, minimizing discomfort. Depot or projectile injections create a controlled release of medication by propelling a dose into the skin, offering sustained effects. Each type enhances patient comfort and compliance while reducing the risk of needle-related injuries.</p></p>

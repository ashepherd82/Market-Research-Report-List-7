<p><p>The coreless DC motors market encompasses two primary types: cylindrical motors and disc motors. Cylindrical motors feature a cylindrical shape with windings directly on the rotor, offering high torque and efficiency in compact designs. They are commonly used in applications requiring precision and responsiveness. Disc motors, on the other hand, have a flat, disc-like structure that allows for lightweight and low-profile designs, making them ideal for applications in robotics and portable devices. Both types cater to specific industry needs, enhancing performance and efficiency.</p></p>

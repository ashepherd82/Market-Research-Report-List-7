<p><p>The bifacial module market is segmented into P-Type and N-Type categories based on the type of silicon used. P-Type modules, utilizing boron-doped silicon, are widely accepted and cost-effective, offering good performance in various conditions. In contrast, N-Type modules, made with phosphorus-doped silicon, provide higher efficiency, better performance in low-light conditions, and reduced degradation over time. While P-Type holds a larger market share, N-Type's advantages are driving growth and interest, appealing to those seeking advanced solar technology.</p></p>

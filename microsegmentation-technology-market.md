<p><p>Microsegmentation technology involves the practice of dividing networks into smaller, isolated segments to enhance security and control. This approach allows organizations to create fine-grained security policies tailored to specific workloads, applications, or user groups, significantly reducing the attack surface. By limiting lateral movement within a network, microsegmentation helps protect sensitive data and critical infrastructure from potential breaches.</p><p>The Microsegmentation Technology Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10% during the forecast period.

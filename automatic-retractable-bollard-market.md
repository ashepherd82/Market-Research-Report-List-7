<p><p>The automatic retractable bollard market is primarily categorized into hydraulic and electric types. Hydraulic bollards utilize fluid pressure systems, allowing for robust installation and high levels of security, making them ideal for heavy-traffic areas. Electric bollards rely on motorized mechanisms for operation, offering easier installation and lower maintenance costs, often suited for lighter-duty applications. Both types enhance traffic control, improve safety, and facilitate access management in various environments, including urban centers, commercial spaces, and restricted zones.</p></p>
